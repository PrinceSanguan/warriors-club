DISCIPLINES

Kids BJJ Class (Warrior Club)

Through structured classes and modern training concepts, Warrior Club helps children harness their physical and mental capabilities, boosting confidence and self-esteem. Classes incorporate both Gi and No-Gi training, introducing kids to the vibrant world of combat sports and martial arts.

Head Coach: Sam Townend
A brown belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu with 9 years of experience, Sam has competed in renowned tournaments and has 2 years of experience coaching children.

Kids BJJ Class (IMA BJJ)

At 11 years old, children can transition into the IMA Academy, a nationally recognized club fostering skill and character development. This structured path enhances techniques while maintaining enthusiasm for martial arts.

Head Coach: Sam Townend
A brown belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Sam is certified in Safeguarding in Martial Arts, First Aid, and holds BMABA qualifications.

Adults No-gi Classes

Train with expert instructors and improve your grappling skills in our adults' No-Gi classes. Suitable for both beginners and advanced practitioners, these sessions provide excellent coaching in a supportive environment.
